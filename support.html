Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

After installing the app, the onboarding guide will walk you through selecting a folder as your document library. This can be a local folder or a cloud-synced folder (iCloud Drive, Dropbox, etc.). Once set up, you can scan or import documents and the app will automatically classify and file them.

Which devices are supported?

ArchiveSorter requires iOS/iPadOS 26.0 or later and runs on iPhone 11 and later as well as all supported iPads.

Where are my documents stored?

All documents are stored in the library folder you selected during setup. The app never uploads your documents to any server. If you chose a cloud-synced folder (e.g. iCloud Drive), synchronization is handled by that cloud provider, not by ArchiveSorter.

How does the AI classification work?

ArchiveSorter uses on-device language models (Apple MLX or Foundation Models) to read your scanned text and assign a category. All processing happens locally on your device. Optionally, you can connect a local LM Studio server on your own network for classification.

Can I change how a document was classified?

Yes. Documents that the app is unsure about are placed in the Review tab for your manual decision. You can also move any document to a different category folder at any time using the app or the Files app.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Scanning, OCR, and on-device classification work entirely without an internet connection. An internet connection is only needed if you choose to download an MLX model from Hugging Face.

How do I delete a document permanently?

Deleted documents are first moved to the app's trash folder. Documents in trash are automatically purged after 30 days, or you can empty the trash manually.
